## **README – Power BI: E-commerce Dashboard**

**Author: Katarzyna Brzeski**  
 **4th-year Informatics student, AI & Data Science specialization**

### **Project Goal**

This project involved building an interactive Power BI dashboard based on sales data from an e-commerce platform. The analysis focused on key sales metrics (KPIs), discounts, payment methods, customer locations, and demographics.

### **Scope of Analysis**

* Data: 55,000 transactions (Kaggle DSV dataset)

* Key columns: Customer ID, transaction ID, gender, age group, product category, discounts, location, payment method

* Workflow:

  * Data cleaning and transformation

  * Created additional columns (year, month, quarter)

  * DAX Measures: average basket value, net sales, transaction count, customer count, etc.

  * Dashboard divided into 6 themed cards

### **Key Insights**

* The 25–45 age group is the most valuable customer segment

* Credit Card is the dominant payment method (40%)

* Electronics and Clothing generate over 50% of total sales

* Discounts boost transaction count but reduce average basket size

* A visible decline in sales in 2024 suggests need for marketing evaluation
